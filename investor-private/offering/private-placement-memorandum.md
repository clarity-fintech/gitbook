# Private Placement Memorandum

PRIVATE PLACEMENT MEMORANDUM Volkov Intelligence Systems L.L.C.

Offering of $CLRTY Network Utility Tokens Date: June 19, 2026 THIS PRIVATE PLACEMENT MEMORANDUM (THE "MEMORANDUM") IS FOR INFORMATIONAL PURPOSES ONLY AND DOES NOT CONSTITUTE AN OFFER TO SELL OR A SOLICITATION OF AN OFFER TO BUY ANY SECURITIES.

1.

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY Volkov Intelligence Systems L.L.C.

("Company") is developing the $CLRTY Network , a deterministic, high-performance blockchain infrastructure designed for institutional settlement and capital management.

The $CLRTY token is a utility instrument serving as the circulatory fuel for the network’s execution layers and the MIRRA Dark Pool.

2.

THE OFFERING The Company is offering early access to the $CLRTY ecosystem to a select group of accredited investors.

This offering is conducted in reliance upon exemptions from the registration requirements of the U.S.

Securities Act of 1933, as amended (the "Securities Act"), including Regulation D.

3.

PROJECT OVERVIEW ● Infrastructure: The network is built on a hard-kernel substrate designed for sub-millisecond finality.

● Tokenomics: The total supply is strictly capped at 16,000,000 $CLRTY tokens.

● Compliance: The architecture features "Compliance-by-Design," integrating FATF-aligned AML/KYC attestation at the protocol layer to meet institutional standards.

● Utility: Tokens function as the primary utility for governance, node registration, and licensing for parallel execution environments.

4.

RISK FACTORS Investing in digital assets involves a high degree of risk.

Investors should consider the following: ● Regulatory Risk: The legal status of digital assets is subject to ongoing scrutiny by global regulators, including the SEC, FATF, and other competent authorities.

● Technological Risk: The $CLRTY protocol relies on novel, deterministic compute environments that may be subject to unforeseen technical vulnerabilities.

● Market Risk: The value of the $CLRTY token is inherently volatile and subject to market liquidity dynamics, including the performance of the MIRRA Dark Pool.

● Operational Risk: The Company’s reliance on multi-agent execution frameworks and multi-signature governance models may introduce centralization risks.

5.

USE OF PROCEEDS Proceeds from this offering will be deployed to accelerate the development of: 1.

Core Kernel Infrastructure: Completing the MVM (Moniverse Virtual Machine) optimization.

1.

Compliance Integration: Deploying the automated Attestation Blob gateway.

1.

Liquidity Provisioning: Seeding the MIRRA Dark Pool for institutional execution.

1.

Operational Reserve: Funding the 177-day development window to TGE.

6.

SUITABILITY OF INVESTORS This offering is limited to "Accredited Investors" (as defined under Rule 501 of Regulation D).

Prospective investors must demonstrate the financial sophistication necessary to understand the risks of early-stage protocol infrastructure.

7.

GOVERNANCE & GOVERNING LAW The $CLRTY network is governed by the project's immutable smart contract architecture, with oversight provided by the Company and delegated governance councils.

All disputes arising under this Memorandum shall be resolved through binding arbitration in [Insert Jurisdiction].

DISCLAIMER: This Memorandum is not a prospectus and has not been reviewed by any regulatory authority.

Prospective investors are urged to conduct their own independent due diligence.

8.

COMPLIANCE-AS-CODE & PROTOCOL-LEVEL OVERSIGHT 8.1.

Integrated AML/KYC Attestation The $CLRTY network incorporates a proprietary "Attestation Blob" protocol at the infrastructure layer.

This ensures that every transaction is cryptographically linked to a verified identity and compliance profile, fulfilling international "Travel Rule" requirements for digital asset transfers.

8.2.

Deterministic Execution & Auditability Unlike legacy DeFi protocols, the $CLRTY network operates on a deterministic, high-performance kernel that produces immutable logs of every state transition.

These logs are anchored to the EntropyBus λ- heartbeat, providing regulators and institutional auditors with real-time, verifiable proof of transaction history, compliance status, and treasury integrity.

8.3.

Automated Governance & Circuit Breakers To protect institutional capital from "Black Swan" market events, the protocol features autonomous, logic-based circuit breakers.

These automated mechanisms are designed to halt trading or liquidity provision within the MIRRA Dark Pool if anomalous volatility or systemic risk signatures are detected, ensuring the network can pause operation until stability is restored by the protocol’s governance councils.

8.4.

Institutional Custody & Multi-Signature Enforcement Treasury and liquidity management are conducted exclusively through Multi-Signature (M-of-N) custody protocols.

This multi-factor requirement eliminates single-point-of-failure vulnerabilities, ensuring that no individual officer of Volkov Intelligence Systems L.L.C.

possesses the administrative permission to unilaterally transfer assets or alter the network’s core parameters.

To finalize the disclosure requirements for your Private Placement Memorandum (PPM), you should include a section dedicated to the "Governance of the MIRRA Dark Pool & Liquidity Management," as this represents a critical component of your institutional value proposition.

9.

GOVERNANCE OF THE MIRRA DARK POOL & LIQUIDITY MANAGEMENT 9.1.

Institutional Dark Pool Architecture The MIRRA Dark Pool is architected to provide institutional participants with high-frequency, private execution environments while maintaining full regulatory auditability.

Order-flow privacy is achieved through encrypted state-transition layers, which protect the integrity of large-block trades from front-running or MEV (Miner-Extractable Value) exploitation.

9.2.

Liquidity Resilience & Slippage Controls To protect capital reserves from market instability, the liquidity management framework employs hard-coded slippage gates.

These primitives enforce maximum price impact limits on all orders, preventing individual transactions from creating localized market distortions.

9.3.

Capital Deployment & Treasury Oversight All liquidity deployed within the MIRRA environment is subject to real-time reconciliation against the project’s total asset supply checksums.

This ensures that treasury balances remain strictly aligned with the protocol's validated state, preventing liquidity fragmentation or unauthorized rehypothecation of assets.

9.4.

Adaptive Protocol Parameters The protocol retains the ability to adapt its liquidity parameters in response to shifting market regimes.

Such adjustments are determined by algorithmic monitoring of orderbook depth and latency, and are subject to the project’s multi-signature governance oversight to ensure institutional-grade accountability.

Strategic Integration Note This section addresses the primary concern of institutional investors: "How is my capital protected while participating in a dark pool?".

By detailing the slippage controls and reconciliation logic, you demonstrate a "security-first" approach that directly addresses the risks associated with high-frequency institutional trading.

Investor Representation The Investor represents and warrants that: ● They qualify as an "Accredited Investor" under Rule 501 of the Securities Act of 1933.

● They possess sufficient knowledge and experience in financial and business matters to evaluate the merits and risks of an investment in the $CLRTY network.

● They are acquiring the interest for their own account for investment purposes only and not with a view toward any resale or distribution.

10.2.

Acknowledgment of Risk The Investor expressly acknowledges that an investment in the Company involves a high degree of risk, including the possible loss of the entire investment.

The Investor confirms that they have had the opportunity to ask questions of and receive answers from the Company concerning the terms and conditions of the offering and have been granted access to the project's technical documentation and due-diligence data room.
