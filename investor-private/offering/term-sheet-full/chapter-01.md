# Term Sheet Full — Chapter 1

Term Sheet and Draft Executable Subscription Framework for the Seed Investment Offering of Volkov Intelligence Systems L.L.C.

Relating to the CLRTY / CLRTY-1 Ecosystem Document Status: Editable for application, diligence, and discussion purposes only.

Prepared For: Current seed financing / early-access institutional raise.

Target Raise: US $1,200,000 - 1,800,000 with an acceptable target range of US$1,500,000 to US$2,000,000.

Date: 2026-07-07.

This Term Sheet and Draft Executable Subscription Framework (this “Term Sheet”) summarizes the principal proposed terms on which Volkov Intelligence Systems L.L.C., a limited liability company organized under applicable law (“VIS” or the “Company”), intends to conduct the current seed investment offering to support development, deployment, compliance readiness, and market launch preparation for the CLRTY / CLRTY-1 ecosystem.

The Company’s materials describe CLRTY as an execution and coordination layer for digital-asset infrastructure, with a fixed 16,000,000 token hard cap, deterministic-finality messaging, treasury controls, multi-signature governance, and institutional onboarding architecture.

Clarity Fintech Market Position Whitepaper Ecosystem Vision Whitepaper This document is intended to satisfy the application requirement for a term sheet or Editable executable document for the current offering.

It is designed to function as the principal summary of the current deal’s economic terms, subscription mechanics, investor qualification requirements, payment flow, and allocation framework.

Final definitive agreements, including any SAFE, SAFT, subscription agreement, side letter, or investor rights instrument, remain subject to legal review, regulatory analysis, final cap-table confirmation, and formal approval by the Company and its counsel.

Private Placement Memorandum III.

Issuer, Offering Structure, and Purpose The issuer is Volkov Intelligence Systems L.L.C., which is developing the CLRTY Network and related components described in public and internal materials, including CLRTY-1, PRISM, HELIX, Fortress, and related infrastructure.

Public and uploaded materials frame the business as deterministic, execution-oriented, institution-facing infrastructure with emphasis on governance controls, supply discipline, liquidity architecture, compliance integration, and developer/operator tooling.

Clarity Fintech Ecosystem Vision Whitepaper Computational Substrate The purpose of this financing is to provide working and growth capital to accelerate the Company’s stack, including protocol hardening, main infrastructure deployment, compliance workflows, liquidity and market structure preparation, operational reserve, and the 177-day development / launch preparation window referenced in Company offering materials.

Private Placement Memorandum The contemplated financing instrument is intended to be flexible enough to satisfy current application requirements while preserving final legal structuring options.

Accordingly, the Company may document this round as: (1) a SAFE with token side-letter mechanics; (2) a SAFT-style subscription with ancillary equity or governance rights; or (3) a hybrid “convertible utility rights” framework in which economic participation is documented contractually and settled into the appropriate post-closing instruments.

The final structure shall be selected by the Company in consultation with qualified counsel and based on securities, commodities, tax, and digital-asset regulatory considerations.

Regulatory Opinion Memo Private Placement Memorandum IV.

Economic Terms of the Seed Raise The Company is seeking US$1,800,000 in gross proceeds in the current raise, with a stated working target range of US$1,500,000 to US$2,000,000.

The proposed post-money valuation cap is US$12,000,000.

The Company may, in its discretion, accept subscriptions above or below the target on a best-efforts basis, provided the final accepted amount remains consistent with cap-table integrity, offering documentation, and applicable law.

The Company’s materials support a capital-backed rather than hype-driven launch posture, emphasizing treasury strength, staged float management, pre-qualified demand, and depth over immediate uncontrolled circulation.

One public-facing planning document describes capital being raised before and at genesis into a multisig treasury, with vested allocations rather than an unmanaged “free float dump,” and frames the funded scenario as one that buys operational depth and runway rather than immunity from volatility.

Notion CLRTY Page No statement in this Term Sheet shall be construed as a promise of appreciation, yield, liquidity, or exchange listing.

Any statements regarding protocol capacity, treasury design, or market structure are included solely to describe business rationale, use of proceeds, and operating intentions.

Regulatory Opinion Memo V.

Token Utility, Ecosystem Context, and Rights Framing The Company’s materials characterize $CLRTY as a utility-oriented computational asset used as execution fuel, for validator bonding or staking, node registration, metered access, governance-adjacent functions, and licensing or access to execution environments.

The system is also described as integrating components such as HELIX for private execution, PRISM as the decision/interface layer, Fortress as a builder or onboarding environment, and NeuroStable as an extended economic stability layer.

Ecosystem Vision Whitepaper Private Placement Memorandum The current draft investor-rights framing is as follows: each accepted investor shall receive a contractual right documented in the definitive agreements to either (a) future equity or SAFE conversion rights, (b) future token-linked or utility-linked allocation rights, or (c) a combined package, depending on the ultimate offering format selected by the Company.

Any token-related rights shall remain subject to legal permissibility, launch conditions, vesting, compliance clearance, and definitive documentation.

Company materials repeatedly refer to a fixed 16,000,000 CLRTY hard cap, with mint authority permanently disabled or null at genesis and no post-genesis minting.

That supply discipline is central to the project’s economic and diligence narrative and should be treated as a foundational offering assumption in the final version of the definitive documents.

Computational Substrate Regulatory Opinion Memo Market Position Whitepaper VI.

Use of Proceeds Net proceeds of the Offering are expected to be applied substantially as follows: First, to core kernel infrastructure and protocol hardening, including continued build-out and optimization of CLRTY-1 and related execution architecture.

Second, to compliance integration, including attestation, identity, KYC/AML workflow implementation, settlement gatekeeping, and institutional-readiness infrastructure.

Third, to liquidity and market-structure provisioning, including launch preparation, treasury-backed depth architecture, and, if and when legally and operationally appropriate, liquidity deployment or market-making support.

Fourth, to operational reserve and launch runway, including the development window leading to TGE, vendor costs, security, legal, and infrastructure operations.

Private Placement Memorandum Market Position Whitepaper To the extent the total accepted raise is below the target amount, the Company intends to prioritize infrastructure, security, compliance, and launch-critical functionality first.

If the raise lands above the base threshold, the Company may accelerate liquidity, partner onboarding, exchange-readiness workstreams, or ecosystem expansion.

This is consistent with the Company’s “anti-fragile” positioning and multi-scenario execution language.
