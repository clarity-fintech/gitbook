EXT (PROCESSES OF PAY) Multi option (All currency forms) We utilize an Atomic, Protocol-Native Subscription Model .

We have removed all intermediary portals by decentralizing the capital commitment process.

Institutional investors interface directly with our secure Subscription Smart Contracts.

Accreditation is verified via real-time on-chain identity oracles, and capital deployment is executed as an atomic transaction—simultaneously signing the digital SAFT and initiating the allocation.

This eliminates all administrative friction, providing our partners with immediate, trustless confirmation of their stake.

The "Protocol-Native" Subscription Model In this model, the "portal" is replaced by a direct interaction with the $CLRTY infrastructure.

1.

Direct-to-Protocol Commitment: The investor interfaces directly with a Subscription Smart Contract hosted on a verifiable, public-facing interface or their own secure dashboard.

There is no "portal" to navigate—they simply connect a wallet (e.g., Fireblocks, Gnosis, or Metamask) to the protocol address.

2.

Embedded Compliance: The compliance check is performed by an on-chain "Identity Oracle" (e.g., a service like Synaps or similar institutional KYC-attestation providers).

○ Mechanism: The investor’s wallet already holds a non-transferable, encrypted "Accreditation Token" (or similar zero-knowledge proof).

○ Result: The subscription contract automatically verifies their accredited status the moment they connect, granting them immediate eligibility to sign.

3.

Atomic Execution: The investor executes the SAFT signature and sends the capital (stablecoin) as part of a single, atomic transaction.

○ Result: The contract automatically records the commitment, anchors the SAFT, and assigns the vesting schedule in the same block.

Why this is the "Ultimate" Ease-of-Use: ● Zero-Interaction Onboarding: There is no "log-in" process.

The investor's wallet is their profile, identity, and signature.

● Platform-Agnostic: This can be embedded into any developer environment, IDE, or terminal interface, allowing investors to commit capital directly from the tools they are already using.

● Total Transparency: Because the investment is an on-chain interaction, the investor has an immutable record of their commitment immediately, without needing your team to manually update any spreadsheets or dashboards.
